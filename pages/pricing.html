Questions
Pricing FAQ.
Can I pay during the class instead of before? ▾
Yes — absolutely. When booking, simply select "Pay during class" and your spot is fully reserved. You settle with the tutor directly at the start of the session, cash or MoMo both welcome.
Do bundle sessions expire? ▾
4-session bundles are valid for 30 days from purchase. 8-session bundles are valid for 60 days. If you need an extension for any reason, just contact us — we're flexible.
Can I mix English and Science in a bundle? ▾
Yes! Bundle sessions can be split freely between English and Science across any grade level (JHS 1, 2 or 3). You can also mix 30-minute and 1-hour sessions within a bundle.
What if I need to cancel or reschedule? ▾
No problem — just contact us at least 3 hours before the session and we'll reschedule at no charge. Cancellations within 3 hours of the session may use one bundle credit.
Are prices the same for online and in-person? ▾
Yes — the same price applies whether you book online or in-person. T&Cs apply to in-person sessions.
