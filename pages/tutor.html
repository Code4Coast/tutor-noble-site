🎯 Teaching Philosophy

My teaching philosophy is built on three core principles:

  • Personalized Learning: Every student learns differently. I adapt my teaching methods to match each student's unique learning style and pace.
  • Building Confidence: Academic success starts with self-belief. I create a supportive environment where students feel comfortable asking questions and making mistakes.
  • Practical Application: I connect classroom concepts to real-world situations, making learning more relevant and memorable for students.
